Kabaddi Information

What is Kabaddi?

Kabaddi is a traditional Indian sport that is played between two teams. It is a contact sport that requires both offensive and defensive skills. The game is played on a rectangular field with each team taking turns to send a "raider" into the opponent's half, while the other team tries to tag the raider and prevent them from returning to their side.

Kabaddi Rules

  • The raider must touch as many opponents as possible and return to their half before being tagged.
  • The defenders must work together to stop the raider and tag them before they escape.
  • Points are scored when a raider successfully touches opponents and returns to their half.
  • The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
